# Image Data

This folder contains 50 real images and 50 AI generated images.

# Data Composition

The real images fall into 5 broad categories: nature, animals, people, food, and city.

The AI images are sorted by chronology: first GANs, and then diffusion models over the years.

## Note on Copyright

I tried to ensure that the images used are free from copyright restrictions. However, finding images is hard and I may have missed some details! If your copyright image is used in this dataset, please contact me and I will replace it from the dataset.
